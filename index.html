How to Create a New Ready-to-Use Account

  1. Click Create New Account.
  2. Wait for the account to load completely.
  3. Click Unlock Account to skip introductions and tutorials.
  4. Click Reload Current Account to apply the unlock changes.
  5. Note: The Realm portals may still appear locked. Use the Level Loader feature as a temporary workaround to access those realms.
